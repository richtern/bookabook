About Us

Bookabook buys and sells used textbooks. We have a shop on Northeastern campus where students pick up the books they purchase from our website. We will buy used books at our location on campus for a good price.

How do I get my books?

You can pick up your books at our convenient location on 123 Address Street. We are open from 11-7 daily.

How do I sell my books?

You can bring the textbooks you want to sell to our location at 123 Address Street. We will look at your books and offer you a price based on their condition.

Do you rent books as well

Yes! We rent books out for a lower price than then a final purchase. You can pick up rented books at our store.

Can I buy books in the store?

Yes! You can order books online or come into our store and buy them there. We accept payment online or in store.
